About Us

50+ Years of Combined Experience in Battery Storage & Renewable Energy

We are a team of TÜV-certified experts with a proven track record in developing major Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) across Europe. With over 50 years of combined leadership in electrical engineering, project development, and consulting, we deliver end-to-end solutions — from EPC to strategic advisory.

Direct experience with utility-scale BESS projects in Germany (up to 250MW / 800MWh)

Trusted by TSOs, DSOs, and global tech leaders like Siemens and SMA

Strong expertise in commercial, technical, and operational project management
